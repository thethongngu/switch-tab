üìñ How It Works

  • The extension tracks your tab usage and maintains a "Most Recently Used" (MRU) list
  • Press Alt+Q repeatedly to cycle through your recently used tabs
  • Press Alt+W to cycle in the opposite direction
  • Release the keys to switch to the selected tab
  • Works similar to Alt+Tab for switching windows in your operating system

üí° Tips

  • Click the extension icon to see your MRU tab list
  • The most recently used tab is always at the top
  • You can click any tab in the popup to switch to it directly
  • The shortcuts work across all Chrome windows
